Plum cake recipe | How to make wine cake at home

Recipe by: Arin Aura

Ingredients:

  • All purpose flour -500 gms
  • Eggs- 10 (room temperature)
  • Unsalted butter- 400 gms (room temperature)
  • Ghee 3-4 tbsp + 5-4 tsp
  • Orange peel/candy peel- 20 gms
  • Powder sugar- 500 gms
  • Dates- 500 gms
  • Cherries- 100 gms
  • Black Raisins–100 gms
  • Cashew- 100 gms
  • Almonds- 100 gms
  • Baking powder- 3tbsp
  • Vanilla essence- 2tsp
  • Semolina- 2tsp
  • Sugar-½ cup
  • Little milk/wine to loosen batter
  • Wine – 187 ml – soak 1-2 days or for a few weeks
  • Cinnamon- 7 gms
  • Clove- 8
  • Nutmeg- 1/2

Method:

1. Fine chop all the dry fruits and soak in wine for at least 2 days (I usually soak for 1 or 2 weeks)
2. Sift AP flour and baking powder
3. Mix butter and sugar to a fine paste in a big mixing bowl
4. Spice mix-Grind cinnamon, clove, nutmeg to a fine powder (grind with sugar for fine powder)
5. Preheat oven to 350°F
6 Put ½ cup sugar in a sauce pan. Add a tsp of water and let the sugar melt and bubble up to turn dark brown. (Once it is brown enough switch off the flame and add some warm/hot water to this, since it cools down and turns solid). Keep this caramelized sugar aside.
7. Separate egg white and yolk
8. Beat egg white to lather to peaks
9. Add egg yolk to the butter sugar paste. Add to this: 3-4 tbsp ghee, beaten egg whites and vanilla essence.
10. Add AP flour and baking powder (2) little at a time and mix.
11. Add to this: 5-4 tsp ghee, caramelized sugar, spice mix and semolina.
12. Loosen the batter with milk/wine if required.
13. Mix the soaked dry fruits.
14. Grease and line the baking pans
15. Bake at 180°C (350°F) on top rack for 45 – 75 mins.

Chicken Nuggets | How to make healthy chicken nuggets at home

Recipe by: Arin Aura
Top

Ingredients:

  • Chicken (chopped,boneless) – 500 gms(approx)
  • Eggs –2
  • Oregano-1 tsp
  • Black pepper powder- ½ tsp
  • Garlic powder – ½ tsp
  • Salt – to taste
  • Paprika – ½ tsp (you can use kashmiri red chili powder if you don’t have paprika)
  • Oil – to deep fry
  • For the coating:
  • Oats – 2-3 cups
  • Salt- 1 tsp
  • Black pepper powder- 1 tsp

Method:

1. chop chicken to nugget size
2. marinate 30 mins in egg, salt, garlic, pepper, paprika, oregano
3. hand crush oats and add salt and pepper to it.
4. Coat the chicken with the oats mix and lay on a plate.
5. Spray each nugget generously with oil to cover the top
6. now place them on the air fry tray… laying the oil side down. spay oil generously on this side too.
7. preheat -air fry at 370F n set time to 14 mins.
8. insert both air fry n drip tray.
9. After about 6 mins flip each piece and again spray oil if needed.
10. turn the tray 180 degree and continue to cook for the remaining time.

Chocolate cookies| How to make whole wheat chocolate cookies at home

Recipe by: Arin Aura
Top

Ingredients:

  • White Sugar- 65 gms (approx 1/3 cup)
  • Brown Sugar-130 gms (approx 2/3 cup)
  • Butter (room temp)- 1 stick (½ cup = 113 g )
  • Egg (room temp)- 1
  • Vanilla Extract – 1 tsp
  • Whole wheat flour – 210 gms (approx 1& 2/3 cups)
  • Baking Soda- 1 tsp
  • Salt – ½ tsp
  • Cocoa Powder – 2 tbsps

Method:

Preheat oven to 190°c| 375°F
1. In a mixing bowl whisk butter and sugar until light and pale
2. Add egg & vanilla extract: beat to incorporate
3. Mix flour, baking soda, cocoa powder & salt in separate bowl
4. Add dry ingredients to wet little at a time
5. chill dough for 24 hours before baking
6. Roll about 2 tbsp dough into balls and place on lined baking sheet.
7. Bake for 7-10 mins or until edges begin to brown
8. Leave the cookies to cool
