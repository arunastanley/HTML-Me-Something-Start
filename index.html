Plum cake recipe | How to make wine cake at home

Recipe by: Arin Aura

Ingredients:

  • All purpose flour -500 gms
  • Eggs- 10 (room temperature)
  • Unsalted butter- 400 gms (room temperature)
  • Ghee 3-4 tbsp + 5-4 tsp
  • Orange peel/candy peel- 20 gms
  • Powder sugar- 500 gms
  • Dates- 500 gms
  • Cherries- 100 gms
  • Black Raisins–100 gms
  • Cashew- 100 gms
  • Almonds- 100 gms
  • Baking powder- 3tbsp
  • Vanilla essence- 2tsp
  • Semolina- 2tsp
  • Sugar-½ cup
  • Little milk/wine to loosen batter
  • Wine – 187 ml – soak 1-2 days or for a few weeks
  • Cinnamon- 7 gms
  • Clove- 8
  • Nutmeg- 1/2

Method:

1. Fine chop all the dry fruits and soak in wine for at least 2 days (I usually soak for 1 or 2 weeks)
2. Sift AP flour and baking powder
3. Mix butter and sugar to a fine paste in a big mixing bowl
4. Spice mix-Grind cinnamon, clove, nutmeg to a fine powder (grind with sugar for fine powder)
5. Preheat oven to 350°F
6 Put ½ cup sugar in a sauce pan. Add a tsp of water and let the sugar melt and bubble up to turn dark brown. (Once it is brown enough switch off the flame and add some warm/hot water to this, since it cools down and turns solid). Keep this caramelized sugar aside.
7. Separate egg white and yolk
8. Beat egg white to lather to peaks
9. Add egg yolk to the butter sugar paste. Add to this: 3-4 tbsp ghee, beaten egg whites and vanilla essence.
10. Add AP flour and baking powder (2) little at a time and mix.
11. Add to this: 5-4 tsp ghee, caramelized sugar, spice mix and semolina.
12. Loosen the batter with milk/wine if required.
13. Mix the soaked dry fruits.
14. Grease and line the baking pans
15. Bake at 180°C (350°F) on top rack for 45 – 75 mins.
